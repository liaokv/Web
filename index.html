Hello! My name is Kevin Liao (pronounced Lee-Ow).

I'm an undergraduate at the University of Maryland Baltimore County, set to graduate in Spring 2016 with a B.S. in Biological Sciences and a minor in Professional Writing.

I have a penchant for teaching and can be found yelling at the students who have had the misfortune of being placed in my genetics discussion class. Additionally, I am a medical scribe (in training) for the ER department of the Shady Grove Medical Center located in Rockville, MD.
