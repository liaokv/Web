ABOUT ME

Hello! My name is Kevin Liao (pronounced Lee-Ow).

I'm an undergraduate at the University of Maryland Baltimore County, set to graduate in Spring 2016 with a B.S. in Biological Sciences and a minor in Professional Writing.

Eventually, I intend to enter Medical School and become a medical doctor. However, if that doesn't work out, there's always professional homelessness.
